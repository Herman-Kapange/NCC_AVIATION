Frequently Asked Questions..

Are there any medical restrictions?

Pilots experience significant G-force during aerobatics and are thus required to be in good health condition and require a medical certificate.

Do I need training?

Most definitely, both new and experienced aerobatic pilots require training from a flight instructor to build their skills. Attempting aerobatics without any training can be disastrous.

What aircraft are used in aerobatics?

The aircraft used in aerobatics have specialised frames that are able to cope with the massive stress of aerobatic maneuvers. Standard aircraft cannot be used!

Is there an age restriction for aerobatics?

Yes, depending on where you are, age restrictions can vary, but generally the minimum age is 12 years old and there is no maximum age.

What Maneuvers can I fly?

The basic aerobatics maneuvers flown are spins, loops, rolls and some combinations of these.

What physical effects will I experience?

Aerobatics involve intense forces and visual orientations that can cause disorientation and anxiety. Tolerance to this will build with more experience.
