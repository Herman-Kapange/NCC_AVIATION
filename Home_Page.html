Welcome To The NCC Aerobatic Aviation Website!

Aerobatics

Aerobatics is a practise in which aircraft are made to perform special maneuvers that are not performed during normal flight.

Aircraft

The maneuvers can be performed in some special airplanes, gliders and helicopters as standard aicraft cannot cope with the stress of the maneuvers.

Purpose

Aerobatics are performed for sport, recreation, entertainment and training.



About

The aim of NCC Aerobatic aviation and this website is to provide guidance, help and support to all aviation enthusiasts at large and more specifically Aerobatics enthusiasts.
