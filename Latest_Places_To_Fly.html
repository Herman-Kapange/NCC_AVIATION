Latest Places to fly

Cape Town, South Africa

Beautiful Scenery and airspace. It cost about £1,180 for a 40 minute flight including some hours of training.



Whitsundays, Australia

Great tropical climate and nice beaches. It cost approximately £145 for a 30 minute flight.



Auckland, New Zealand

Beautiful weather with flights available all year round. It costs approximately £195 for a 20 mintue flight with 8 stunts.



Los Angeles, USA

Stable weather which facilitates flights all year round> This place offers more of a hands on experience. It costs approximately £220 for a 25 minute flight.
